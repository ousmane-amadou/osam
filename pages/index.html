About Me

Hello world! My name is Ousmane Amadou, but most people call me Oz. I am a self-taught developer and UX designer based in Toronto. I started experimenting with code around 8 years ago. Since then I have worked on a variety of projects including games, mobile apps, and web apps. Recently, I have been doing freelance web development for small businesses and startups. In the Fall, I worked on the front-end of rabbithole for DataX.

I am cognitive science (computational stream) and statistics student at the University of Toronto focusing on Human-Computer Interaction. During my time at the UofT, I have been involved with the ACM-ICPC team, numerous hackathons, and two startup incubation programs. Right now, I am working on something new at The Hatchery Accelerator.

In my free time, I like to get involved in musical performance. I can play a handful of instruments including the guitar trumpet, and the voice. In the past, I have played for jazz bands, choirs, and musicals. These days, however, I by like to DJ part-time for student organizations.
